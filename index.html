Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: What is Kuya JC Fried Chicken?

A: Kuya JC Fried Chicken is a food business under the Franco group that offers a wide range of food and beverage offerings. Its signature fried chicken and Filipino-style dishes are perfect for dine-in, take-out, and delivery.

Q: What is the meaning behind Kuya JC Fried Chicken's slogan "Kanyaman Na"?

A: "Kanyaman Na" is a Kapampangan word that means "delicious." Kuya JC Fried Chicken's owner and founder is Kapampangan himself, and the business is inspired by the province's passion for food.

Q: What services does Kuya JC Fried Chicken offer?

A: Kuya JC Fried Chicken offers food and beverage services, take-out services, and dine-in services. Its cozy restaurants are perfect for a satisfying meal with your friends and family.

Q: Does Kuya JC Fried Chicken offer franchising opportunities?

A: Yes, Kuya JC Fried Chicken offers franchise packages such as the Basic Food Cart Package, Standard Food Cart Package, and Customise Kiosk Package.

Q: What are Kuya JC Fried Chicken's best-seller products?

A: Kuya JC Fried Chicken's best-seller products include Chicken Wings and Fried Chicken Bucket.

Q: How much do Kuya JC Fried Chicken's products cost?

A: Prices range from ₱ 49.00 to ₱ 499.00 depending on the product and combo deals available.

Q: What type of payment do we accept?

A: Cash only.
